# Sample Prompts for Legal RAG System

## Wisconsin Statutes (Chapter 939-968, 175, 165)

### 1. Criminal Elements and Definitions
**Prompt:** "What are the elements required to prove a felony crime under Wisconsin law? What distinguishes a Class A felony from a Class C felony?"

### 2. Homicide and Use of Force
**Prompt:** "Under Wisconsin statute Chapter 940, what is the legal definition of first-degree intentional homicide? When is use of deadly force by a law enforcement officer justified?"

### 3. Property Crimes
**Prompt:** "What constitutes theft under Wisconsin law in Chapter 943? What are the different degrees of theft based on property value?"

### 4. Controlled Substances
**Prompt:** "What are the penalties for possession with intent to deliver controlled substances in Wisconsin under Chapter 961? How are different drug schedules classified?"

## Wisconsin Case Law

### 1. Search and Seizure Precedents
**Prompt:** "What did the Wisconsin Supreme Court rule regarding warrantless searches of vehicles during traffic stops? Cite recent case law."

### 2. Miranda Rights Application
**Prompt:** "How have Wisconsin courts interpreted the requirement to provide Miranda warnings? What constitutes custody for Miranda purposes?"

### 3. Evidence Admissibility
**Prompt:** "What are the Wisconsin court rulings on the admissibility of eyewitness identification evidence? What factors determine reliability?"

### 4. Use of Force Standards
**Prompt:** "What legal standards do Wisconsin courts apply when evaluating law enforcement use of force? Summarize key case precedents."

## Wisconsin Policies (Court Procedures)

### 1. Court of Appeals Procedures
**Prompt:** "What are the filing requirements and procedures for appeals in the Wisconsin Court of Appeals? What are the deadlines for filing briefs?"

### 2. Electronic Filing Rules
**Prompt:** "What are the Wisconsin court rules regarding electronic filing and facsimile submissions? Are there exceptions for law enforcement documents?"

### 3. Appellate Review Process
**Prompt:** "How does the Wisconsin Court of Appeals review criminal convictions? What is the standard of review for factual findings versus legal conclusions?"

### 4. Court Operating Procedures
**Prompt:** "What are the internal operating procedures for the Wisconsin Court of Appeals? How are cases assigned to different districts?"

## Federal Statutes (US Code Title 18, 21, 42)

### 1. Federal Firearms Laws
**Prompt:** "What are the federal requirements for firearm possession under 18 USC Chapter 44? Who is prohibited from possessing firearms?"

### 2. Federal Fraud Statutes
**Prompt:** "What constitutes wire fraud under Title 18? What are the elements the government must prove for a federal fraud conviction?"

### 3. Federal Drug Laws
**Prompt:** "Under Title 21, what are the mandatory minimum sentences for drug trafficking offenses? How do federal drug schedules differ from Wisconsin?"

### 4. Civil Rights Violations
**Prompt:** "What is 42 USC Section 1983 and how does it apply to law enforcement misconduct? What constitutes a constitutional violation under federal civil rights law?"

### 2. Qualified Immunity

### 3. Constitutional Criminal Procedure

## Cross-Category Complex Queries

### 1. Multi-Jurisdictional Analysis
**Prompt:** "Compare Wisconsin state law on controlled substances with federal drug laws. When would a drug offense be prosecuted federally versus in state court?"

### 2. Constitutional Standards
**Prompt:** "How do Wisconsin statutes on search and seizure comply with federal constitutional requirements? What additional protections does Wisconsin provide?"

### 3. Use of Force Comparison
**Prompt:** "Compare Wisconsin state law on police use of force with DOJ federal guidelines. What are the key differences in standards and reporting requirements?"

### 4. Appeals and Review
**Prompt:** "If a defendant is convicted in Wisconsin state court, what is the process for appealing to federal court? When can federal courts review state convictions?"

## Practical Law Enforcement Scenarios

### 1. Traffic Stop Scenario
**Prompt:** "An officer conducts a traffic stop and smells marijuana. Under Wisconsin and federal law, what is the legal basis for searching the vehicle? What evidence can be seized?"

### 2. Arrest and Custody
**Prompt:** "What are the legal requirements under Wisconsin law for making a warrantless arrest? When must an arrestee be brought before a judge?"

### 3. Evidence Collection
**Prompt:** "What are the Wisconsin and federal requirements for collecting and preserving digital evidence from a suspect's phone? Is a warrant always required?"

### 4. Juvenile Offenses
**Prompt:** "How does Wisconsin's Children's Code (Chapter 48) handle juvenile offenders? What are the differences between juvenile and adult criminal procedures?"
